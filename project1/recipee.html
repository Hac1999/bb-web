Delicious Biryani Recipe (1 kg)

Ingredients

  • 4 cups basmati rice
  • 1 kg chicken or vegetables
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 4 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 4 tbsp biryani masala
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 4 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tsp saffron (soaked in warm milk)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the onions until golden brown. Set aside half of them.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste to the pan and saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add the chicken/vegetables, biryani masala, tomatoes, and yogurt. Cook until the meat/vegetables are tender.
  5. In a separate pot, boil water, add soaked rice, and cook until 70% done. Drain the water.
  6. In a large pot, layer half the rice, followed by the cooked chicken/vegetables, and top with the remaining rice.
  7. Garnish with fried onions, saffron milk, coriander, and mint leaves.
  8. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes.
  9. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes before serving.
